DEVAMPED is essentially a simplified bedrock edition texture pack, while at first it started as cartoon-ish/rpg style, along of progress it's changed into something new entirely. it was now reimagined vanilla having both cartoon-ish and retro-rpg style.

It was one of my hobby project that I thought might look cool despite most of my favorite texture packs out there are mainly focused on Java edition. which I can't really use them on bedrock without manually converting them. the converter is also don't work well.

Hence, this project was born. This will not be on marketplace for that exact reason and will remain free like any texture packs out there.

While this was fully made on mobile, developments may go vary. so, it may slow down or sometimes faster. it depends. The current pack percentage are 25% completion, and will get updates by Daily - 3-5 days as usual, or maybe weekly in-case of update taking long or there's accidents. (e.g accidentally deleting the whole assets rhat includes the update, or don't really have time to finish the update.)
